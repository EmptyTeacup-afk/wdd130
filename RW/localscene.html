The Local Scene

The local sport scene will always be the roots for Football CPT. There are weekly amateur matches organized and filmed. This has promoted football on a gross roots level, encouraging people from all walks of life to get involved in the sport and not just in a playing capacity. Whether you are playing, spectating or looking to practice your filming skills!

Tournaments

Football CPT has hosted tournaments in the past. This caters to the more competitive players in the community. Allowing for them to show off their skills and win prizes in the process. The tournaments really showcase what Football CPT is all about, bringing together the amateur football community and providing a platform to not feel so amateur.
