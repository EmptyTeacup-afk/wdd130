Content

Home page

OUR BEGINNING Founded by Celso de Nobrega in 2023, Football CPT originated as a simple idea of filming amateur football matches. The purpose behind this was to capture the essence of friendly football matches, while creating lasting memories for the participants. Over time this vision has evolved, and we are now committed to elevating the local amateur football scene in Cape Town. OUR MISSION At Football CPT, our mission is to foster the growth and development of amateur football by providing a dynamic platform for local players and teams with a greater ‘reason’ to play. Our goal is to create a vibrant community that celebrates the passion for football and connects like-minded individuals who share a common love for the beautiful game. OUR INITIATIVES SOCIAL MEDIA From the start, Football CPT has focused on leveraging the power of social media to grow our community and showcase local talent. EVENTS We take pride in organizing and hosting local events for various teams and players to participate in. These events allow for players, from all walks of life, to participate as well as to form networks within the community. At Football CPT we are committed to providing a platform for football enthusiasts to connect with like-minded people, showcase their abilities and create memories both on and off the pitch.

Rooftop Wanderers

Founded in 2023, Rooftop Wanderers is Football CPT’s official team. Currently playing in the Grand Central league in the heart of the mother city. Nothing beats playing football on a rooftop with Table Mountain in the backdrop! The team was founded to allow local players to showcase their talents on a more competitive stage. Since its founding Rooftop Wanderers has won three league titles in a row and is pushing for its fourth (as of 2025). The team has seen its players come and go with many of them being from the local area. All of them with different background and personalities, truly embracing South Africa’s diversity. Football CPT and Rooftop Wanderers are proud to be involved in supporting CANSA. the vision of the Cancer Association of South Africa (CANSA), is to lead South Africa towards a cancer free society It’s mission as a non-profit fighting cancer, is to enable research, educate the public and advocate for and support all people affected by cancer. The Local Scene

The local sport scene will always be the roots for Football CPT. There are weekly amateur matches organized and filmed. This has promoted football on a gross roots level, encouraging people from all walks of life to get involved in the sport and not just in a playing capacity. Whether you are playing, spectating or looking to practice your filming skills! Tournaments - Football CPT has hosted tournaments in the past. This caters to the more competitive players in the community. Allowing for them to show off their skills and win prizes in the process. The tournaments really showcase what Football CPT is all about, bringing together the amateur football community and providing a platform to not feel so amateur.
